# 💧 Municipal & Community Borehole Utilities Engineering

Welcome to my utilities and mechanical engineering portfolio. This repository documents my practical experience managing, servicing, and restoring large-scale water extraction networks, fluid piping, and heavy pumping machinery—heavily aligned with industrial plant utilities and factory fluid system requirements.

## 🛠️ Core Technical Competencies Demonstrated

### 1. Mechanical Plant & Pump Maintenance
* **Heavy Machinery Handling:** Practical experience uncoupling, rigging, and safely lowering high-capacity submersible borehole pumps into deep well shafts.
* **Asset Calibration:** Routine mechanical servicing, impeller clearance inspections, and mechanical seal replacement to optimize asset longevity and efficiency.

### 2. Fluid Mechanics & Pipeline Plumbing
* **Pressure Regulation:** Installing, checking, and aligning non-return valves, pressure gauges, and isolating gate valves across industrial lines.
* **Friction Loss Mitigation:** Ensuring airtight high-pressure couplings and pipework alignment to completely eliminate leaks and maintain peak hydraulic throughput.

### 3. Electrical Interfacing & Fault Diagnostics
* **Control Loop Integration:** Wiring automated float switches, electronic depth sensors, and low-voltage water level control relays to protect pumps against dry-running.
* **Motor Diagnostics:** Utilizing multi-meters to conduct winding insulation resistance tests and verify phase current balancing on industrial-grade 3-phase pump motors.

### 4. Breakdown Recovery & Reliability
* **Rapid Fault Resolution:** Executing detailed breakdown analyses under high-pressure scenarios to quickly isolate mechanical jams or electrical control failures, successfully averting systemic supply downtime.
